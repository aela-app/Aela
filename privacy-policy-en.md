# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: February 6, 2026  
**Effective Date**: February 6, 2026  
**App Name**: `Aela`

Thank you for using Aela. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
### 1.1 Required Permissions
- Photo Library Access: For importing and saving images. All content and data are **processed locally only, never uploaded to servers**.

### 1.2 Non-Personal Information
- App Usage Data: Limited, aggregated analytics provided by Apple (if enabled on your device) and crash reports to help improve the app. This data is not used to identify you.

### 1.3 Information We Don’t Collect
- **Aela has no user registration system**, so we don’t store personal account information.
- **We don’t upload your images or files**.
- **We don’t collect location information**.
- **We don’t access contacts**.
- **We don’t run background recordings**.

## 2. Data Processing and Storage
- **Local Processing Only**: All image processing occurs on your device.
- **No Cloud Storage**: Aela does not provide any backend service or cloud storage.
- **System Services**: If you enable iCloud Photos at the system level, Apple may sync your media according to Apple’s policies. This is outside of our control.

## 3. Third-Party Services
### Tencent Bugly Crash Reporting Service
- **Service Provider**: Tencent Cloud Computing (Beijing) Co., Ltd.
- **Purpose**: Application crash monitoring and performance analysis
- **Information Collected**:
- Device information (device model, operating system version)
- Application crash logs and stack trace information
- Application version information
- Anonymous device identifiers
- **Data Processing**: Data is transmitted to Tencent Bugly servers for analysis and processing
- **Data Security**: Encrypted transmission is used, and no personal sensitive information is collected

### Apple Services (App Store / In-App Purchase / Analytics)
- **Purpose**: Payments, subscription management, and aggregated usage analytics
- **Processing**: Handled by Apple under their policies. We do not receive your payment details.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Aela is rated **4+**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Your Rights
You may:
- **Access and Correct**: Contact us via email.
- **Delete Data**: Uninstall the app or delete local data from your device.
- **Withdraw Consent**: Disable Photo Library permission (some features may be limited).
